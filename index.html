Circuit Imaging

Circuits at the speed of light

Circuit Imaging is developing direct imaging systems capable of imaging PC boards significantly faster than anything currently on the market. The throughput of our imaging systems is also scalable. In addition to the high throughput, our patented technology includes entry level models that enable film based PCB manufacturing companies to upgrade to direct imaging. Furthermore, each machine is directly connected to Microsoft Azure, enabling real time monitoring and analytics to provide predictive maintenance and auto-repair functionality with each machine leveraging Azure IoT. Finally, with "circuit boards at the speed of light," our system includes patented high-speed board handling processes.
